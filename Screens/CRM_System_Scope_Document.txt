================================================================================
                         EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

The CRM system is designed for SpecCon Holdings, a training and education
services company operating in South Africa (currency: ZAR/Rand). The system
manages B2B client relationships across corporate clients and schools, with
products including Learnerships, TAP Business programs, Compliance training,
and Other Courses.

================================================================================
                 SECTION 7: KEY BUSINESS PROCESSES
================================================================================

1. LEAD-TO-DEAL PIPELINE:
   Lead Generation -> Initial Contact -> Needs Assessment ->
   Proposal Sent -> Negotiation -> Deal Closed

2. QUOTE-TO-INVOICE FLOW:
   Create Quote -> Send -> Approval -> Generate Invoice -> Track Payment

3. CONTRACT LIFECYCLE:
   Draft -> Pending Approval -> Active -> Expiring Soon -> Renewal/Amendment

4. FORECASTING PROCESS:
   Historical data -> YTD actuals -> Monthly forecasting -> Variance analysis

5. CLIENT FEEDBACK LOOP:
   Training completion -> Feedback collection -> Rating analysis ->
   Follow-up actions
